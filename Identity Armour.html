Spot the Deepfake: Real vs. Fake

Learn to identify subtle differences between authentic images and AI-generated deepfakes.

🟢 Real Image

Natural lighting, consistent shadows, organic texture.

🔴 Deepfake Image

Unnatural blending, artifacts, inconsistent expressions.

Key Differences to Watch For

  • Facial Consistency: Natural asymmetries vs mismatched features
  • Lighting & Shadows: Uniform vs mismatched highlights
  • Skin Texture: Organic pores vs overly smooth
  • Edge Artifacts: Sharp vs blurring/ghosting
  • Background: Seamless vs warped
